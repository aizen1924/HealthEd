Cuts & Scrapes

  • Clean with water and mild soap.
  • Apply pressure to stop bleeding.
  • Cover with a clean bandage.

Nosebleed

  • Sit down and lean forward slightly.
  • Pinch the soft part of the nose.
  • Breathe through mouth (10 mins).

Minor Burns

  • Run cool (not cold) water.
  • Do NOT apply ice or oil.
  • Cover loosely with sterile gauze.
