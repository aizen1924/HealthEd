What is HealthEd?

HealthEd is an interactive mobile application designed to simplify health education for students. We bridge the gap between complex medical information and daily life through gamified learning and accessible guides.

Our Mission

To empower the youth with essential knowledge on Hygiene, First Aid, Nutrition, and Mental Health, creating a healthier, safer, and more aware generation.
