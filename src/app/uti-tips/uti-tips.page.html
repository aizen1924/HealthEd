Beat the Bacteria!

UTI (Urinary Tract Infection) is a common infection, but you can stop it before it starts.

Did you know? What is a UTI? UTI happens when bacteria (germs) get into your urinary system. It usually affects the bladder and urethra. It is more common in females, but anyone can get it.

Hydrate

Drink 8-10 glasses. Water dilutes urine and flushes out bacteria.

Wipe Right

Always wipe Front to Back to keep bacteria away from the urethra.

Don't Hold It

Pee when you need to. Holding it lets bacteria grow.

Wear Cotton

Cotton lets skin breathe and stays dry. Bacteria love moisture.

Hygiene Habits

Avoid strong feminine washes or sprays. They can upset your natural balance. Plain water and mild soap are best!
